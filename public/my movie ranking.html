The Best Movie According to ME

My top 3 movies of all time

Up

It is a heartwarming animated adventure where an elderly widower and a young boy fly to South America in a house lifted by balloons.

Wall-E

It is a touching animated film about a lonely waste-collecting robot on a desolate Earth who embarks on a space journey that ultimately decides the fate of humanity.

Nacho Libre

It is a comedy film about a monastery cook who secretly becomes a luchador to raise money for the orphanage where he works.
